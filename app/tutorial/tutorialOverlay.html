  6. Task Search

    Use this to search your tasks. Search or filter just about anything you want people, things you own, tags, photos, videos. You name it, we'll find it. The arrow on the far right will expand and collapse the search box.

  7. Task Search

    Got an email or phone call from a client wanting a status? Use this to search for tasks or clients to quickly update them. Search or filter just about anything you want clients, client assets, tags, photos, videos. You name it, we'll find it. The arrow on the far right will expand and collapse the search box.

  8. We also have two permanent lists, Shared with Me and Tasks. These let you quickly see tasks that others have shared with you and tasks that are not part of any list.

  9. Create a new task

    Create your tasks here. Any task you want. Start typing then click TaskIt! to get started.

  10. Create a new task

    Create your tasks here. Any task you want. Then click TaskIt! to get started. You can also create tasks on behalf of clients? Need to do an oil change and want client approval? Just type the task, click the drop down arrow and you'll create a task on behalf of any client you select.

  11. Your new task

    Click here to expand any task you have created. This will allow you to add photos and videos to any task, assign the task to someone else such as a service provider, add custom search tags and share tasks with friends and family for feedback.

  12. Your new task

    Click here to expand any task you have created. This will allow you to add photos and videos to any task and also view photos and videos clients have uploaded, view client messages, assign the task to a service technician, check work status, view tech notes, add custom search tags, and allow service techs to log hours, parts and expenses associated with a task.
